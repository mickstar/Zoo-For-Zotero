Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is mickstarify's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you through our app, Zoo for Zotero.

Important Note: Zoo for Zotero is a third-party application and has no official relation to Zotero or the Corporation for Digital Scholarship. This app is an independent project that connects to Zotero's public API.

Data Collection

Zoo for Zotero does not automatically collect any personal information. The app only accesses your Zotero library data when you provide your API credentials to sync with your Zotero account. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any third-party services.

Crash Reporting

Crash logs are only collected if you voluntarily choose to send them. The app may prompt you to send crash reports to help improve the software, but this is entirely optional and requires your explicit consent each time.

External Services

The app connects to Zotero's official API (api.zotero.org) to sync your library data. Please refer to Zotero's privacy policy for information about how they handle your data. The app may also link to external sites that are not operated by us. We have no control over the content and practices of these sites.
